## Data safety / App privacy answers (both stores)

Consistent with landing/privacy.html:

- **Account / login**: None.
- **Personal identifiers collected by us**: None (no name/email/phone).
- **Advertising ID**: Collected by Google AdMob for ads. (Play: "Device or
  other IDs" → shared for advertising. Apple: "Identifiers → Device ID →
  Third-Party Advertising".)
- **Diagnostics / analytics**: Anonymous device/usage stats via Firebase
  (not linked to identity).
- **Data stored on device only**: language/theme settings, content cache —
  not transmitted to our servers.
- **Data not sold.** Not directed at children.
