Privacy Policy

K-Drama Hangul · Last updated: July 24, 2026

K-Drama Hangul ("the App") respects your privacy. The App has no sign-up and no login, and does not collect personal information that directly identifies you — such as your name, email address, or phone number.

1. Information stored on your device

The following is stored only on your device and is never sent to our servers:

  • App settings such as your interface language and dark/light theme
  • Downloaded scene content cached for smooth, offline-friendly display

2. Loading content

The App loads its scene and word data from a public repository (GitHub), and plays drama clips through YouTube's official embedded player. Those services' own privacy policies may apply when their content is loaded.

  • YouTube: Google Privacy Policy

3. Advertising

The App displays ads through Google AdMob. To serve ads, AdMob may collect and use certain information such as an advertising identifier (e.g. your device's advertising ID).

  • How Google uses information for ads
  • You can reset or opt out of ad personalization in your device settings.

4. Analytics and remote configuration

The App may use Google Firebase (Remote Config and related services) to improve stability and features. This may process anonymous device and usage statistics that do not identify you personally.

5. Children's privacy

The App is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from them.

6. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated to reflect changes in the law or the service. Any changes will be posted on this page.
